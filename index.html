Amr Morsy

Graphics Engineer



My name is Amr Morsy, and I am a graphics engineer. I specialize in building game engines, games, and real-time graphics systems. I am currently pursuing graduate studies while working as a computer graphics researcher. I write technical blogs on theoretical computer graphics, along with books and tutorials focused on low-level graphics APIs. I also offer tutoring in computer graphics and take on freelance work in graphics programming.
